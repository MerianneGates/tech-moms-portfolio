Experience
Truly, my experience comes from the several years of lessons I took myself, the events and occasions where I was able to play, and the moments at home where I am able to revisit music that I have loved playing. I have taught lessons throughout the last 6 years, but have intentionally kept things very casual and small. I teach because I love my students, and I love being able to share this hobby with them.
